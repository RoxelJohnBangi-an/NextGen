Place your custom images here.

Examples:
- hero-image.png      (Hero section illustration or screenshot)
- logo.png            (Your brand logo)
- og-image.png        (Open Graph / social share preview image, 1200x630px)
- favicon.ico         (Browser tab icon)
- team-photo.jpg      (About / team section)

Reference images in index.html using relative paths:
  <img src="assets/images/hero-image.png" alt="..." />

Tip: Optimize images before adding them (use squoosh.app for compression).
